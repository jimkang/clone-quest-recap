Quest 2: Clones

At the start of Quest 2, the Worst Party breaks out of glass cages in the arena, cross pillars rising out of an acid pool, and defeat umber hulks and eyewings.

But some of the party members are clones.

Meanwhile, the other versions of the party watch from lounges. Some of the watchers are clones, and some are original members of the party. Regardless, they receive ballots and vote for those in the area that they are clones, based on their careful observation.

Most vote, but some abstain.

The arena players voted likely to be clones are Eunice, Awnzo, Nettie, and Ken. And so, their lounge versions are swapped with their arena versions.

They defeat demons, a big spider, and a slippery slope. Then, the arena party reach large, person-sized pods. Time is running out on the violent expiration of the clones.

The Makalbly, Awnzo, Omom, and Ken in the arena enter the pods to swap with their other selves.

The arena party separates and embeds themselves in secret underground chambers. Time finally runs out.

The clones explode!

And thus, Quest 2: Clones is complete!

The Worst Party then embarks on Quest 3: Hells Crawl. They escape from the Hell of the Mountain of Ice, defeat the Citrus Leech, and win the audience away from the Avocado Snail!

Before they move on to the next hell, meta-drama strikes! The show's staff discovers that one of the PCs does not have the soul of a person, and is thus disqualified. The host of the Coolest Mortal offers consolation prizes and teleportation to where they could send the soul.

Their demon-defeating, crowd winning, explosion avoiding, ice climbing, secret hourglass-finding, and other adventuring earned the PCs 106,750 XP!

Will the Worst Party leave "N.H."? Or will they try to continue the Hells Crawl on their own? We shall see next time!
